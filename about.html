About Momentum

Momentum is a personal productivity PWA designed to help you plan your day, track important tasks, and reflect on your progress. Built with a focus on simplicity and effectiveness, it combines task management with daily scoring to keep you motivated and productive.

Whether you're a student, professional, or anyone looking to organize their day better, Momentum provides the tools you need without unnecessary complexity.
